The Childhood

Jethro Vega Tatco a good-looking and a wise young adult in his twenties1. Born in the 18th day of October, 2000 in the city of Pasig, Jethro was known to be the naughtiest among the four (4) children of Analiza and Michael Tatco, which is why if you can see he really has a lot of scars; some of it visible on his face2. As a child, Jethro was fond of playing outside3. He is very social and gained a lot of friends4. Expectedly, those friends of him are still his friends up to this date5. He had all the things that children want back then; bike, PSP, Gameboy, a personal computer, you name it6. He was a very protective brother to his younger sisters, which is very understandable7. Jethro grew up without his older brother by his side, so when he finally did, it was a long ride of quarrel and fist fights8. They fortunately get passed that and became the best of friends9.


His parents were very caring, yet they let him learn how to be independent by letting him discover new things all by himself10. In fact, Jethro is very active in his extracurricular activities, end really excel on them11. He was a part of his elementary school's Boy Scout organization12. He shared how they went camping in Mount Makiling, Mount Capinpin, Baguio and even inside the school13. That is where he learned how to be strategic14. They were taught how to lit fire with sticks and stones and cook chicken with it15. He also experienced competing with other schools in a tug of war, where his team expectedly won16. He remained friends with those people he met on the campings he went17. Jethro also joined their elementary school's Dance Troupe, which focuses on Traditional Dances18.


He also joined basketball leagues within their barangay and of course, he found new friends and even enemies who envy how he plays19. He loved playing basketball ever since20. His uncle taught him how to play it, and ever since, he enjoyed it so much, he dreamt of being a varsity player21.

The Teenage Years

In my teenage years, his life revolves around that dream as well1. He was transferred to a private school with his brother2. Unfortunately, he was bullied before for being a transferee3. Yet, it was not for too long when he met new friends who have the same interest as he did4. They told him to join fraternities so he cannot be bullied, and someone will have his back5. From being the bullied transferee, Jethro quickly became famous for beating the well-known bully in their school on a fist fight6.


But his teenage life did not revolve here.6 He pursued his passion in sports played for their school and compete7. He even joined their volleyball team, and won a lot of competitions as well8. This athletic journey of his did not stop when he moved on to Senior High, where he studied in University of the East9. He was a student of Accounting and Business Management10. They have a workload of tasks, but that did not stop him from playing11. In fact, his passion grew more12. One of his coaches back in Grade 7 admired how he play, and recommended him to play in Lyceum of the Philippines University13. He went for a try-out, and got in14. Unfortunately, after that day, he was injured after playing another game, and he was diagnosed with Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL)15. ACL is a common injury for athletes, and its treatment is very expensive16. His family did not have the money to get him treated, and that is when all his dream of being a varsity player shambles17. As soon as he knew that, he know he cannot play again18. He needed to rest his body for a long time, because one abuse of himself might get him into a deeper injury19. It was unfortunate, but he have no other choice but to accept it20.

College Years

From wanting to be an Architecture, Business Manager, and a varsity player, Jethro resorted into pursuing IT1. It was a stressful journey for him, because he did not have any knowledge at all about this course2. He only chose it because his father recommended it3. Still, he is trying his best to keep up with all the lessons4. However, now he is in college, he always wonders how things would be different if he didn't get injured5. He did not feel all blues, though6. He is still grateful that he gained friends in his college years that helped him in every task he cannot do all by himself7. His professors are also very helpful and understanding, ready to answer all the questions, and ready to explain everything that he did not understand8. Maybe, God really have other plans for him9.


What's bad is his eyesight10. Spending all day in computer, may it be for leisure or academics, really worsen his eyesight11. He cannot go outside and play because it hurts his knees, and there's really no other option for him but to play whenever he's stressed or sad12. Sometimes, he will hold a ball, dribble it and shoot it on the wall13. He did not need to run, jump or anything he just wanted to feel the ball on his hands14. It was really unfortunate, but who knows where this man might go?15. He is a brave man, and he can conquer any problems he will face16. He always had a strong heart, and that is one of his traits that is very admirable17. Yet, having a strong heart does not always necessarily mean that he is strong all the time he cries, gets mad, and sometimes take a step back when he know that he cannot handle everything18. He believes that with the right people to support you and right motivation to move on, he will be destined in the right place19. Jethro is an inspiring man, and every tear he shed now, will water his plants for tomorrow20.
